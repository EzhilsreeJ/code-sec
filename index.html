About Saveetha Engineering College

Saveetha Engineering College (SEC), established in 2001, is a premier institution dedicated to academic excellence and research. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various engineering disciplines. Our commitment is to foster innovation, critical thinking, and a lifelong learning attitude among our students.

Located in the vibrant city of Chennai, SEC boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a dynamic campus environment.

Admissions

We are excited to welcome aspiring students to join our community. Admissions for the upcoming academic year are now open.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • For B.E./B.Tech: A pass in the 10+2 examination with Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects or any other examination of the equivalent.
  • For M.E./M.Tech: A pass in a relevant Bachelor's degree in Engineering/Technology.

For detailed information on programs, eligibility, and the application process, please visit our admissions portal or contact us.
